Extracting investigators' research interests from ORCiD Profiles

The project is to study PI's research interests and provide suggestions on potential funding applications and collaborations at BC Children's Hospital Research Institute. This project includes extract publication information from public ORCID profiles and MeSH Keywords from Pubmed database, visualization and statistical analysis to study research interests and trends, explore research areas for BCCHR researchers in various subsets of the institute
